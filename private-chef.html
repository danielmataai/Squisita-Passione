Your Personal Italian Chef

Transform your home into an authentic Italian restaurant for one unforgettable evening. As your private chef, I bring the passion and flavors of Italy directly to your table, creating a bespoke dining experience tailored to your preferences and occasion.

From intimate dinners for two to elegant gatherings of up to 20 guests, every detail is carefully crafted—from menu creation to table presentation. You and your guests can relax and enjoy while I handle everything in your kitchen.

How It Works

1 Step

Consultation

We discuss your event, preferences, dietary requirements, and create a custom menu together. I'll answer all your questions and plan every detail.

2 Step

Preparation

I source the finest ingredients and prepare what can be done in advance. On the day, I arrive early to set up in your kitchen.

3 Step

The Experience

Sit back and enjoy an evening of exceptional Italian cuisine. Each course is prepared fresh and presented beautifully.

4 Step

Clean Up

After the final course, I take care of the kitchen cleanup, leaving your space spotless. All you need to do is enjoy the memories.
