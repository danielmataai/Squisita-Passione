About Me

Chef Davide Rizzardi

Davide Rizzardi is an experienced chef with 21 years working in different countries behind a kitchen. He is energetic and dynamic, with a bright personality, passionate, creative and capable to cook and create a dish with any available ingredients on hand.

He has a devotion for traditional Italian cuisine that loves to share with his diners and customers, also sharing his knowledge when teaching cooking courses around the world.
